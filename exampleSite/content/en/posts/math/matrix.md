Mathematics contains many useful and elegant formulas. Here are a few examples with short explanations.

## 1. Quadratic Formula

For $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$:

$$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$

## 2. Euler's Formula

$$e^{i\theta} = \cos\theta + i\sin\theta$$

A famous special case is:

$$e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$$

## 3. Basic Definite Integral

$$\int_a^b x^n dx = \left[ \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} \right]_a^b = \frac{b^{n+1}}{n+1} - \frac{a^{n+1}}{n+1}$$

## 4. Expectation and Variance

$$E[X] = \sum_i x_i p_i$$

$$Var(X) = E[(X - E[X])^2] = E[X^2] - (E[X])^2$$
